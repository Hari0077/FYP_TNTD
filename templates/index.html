  What is that Thyroid Nodule Type Detection do?

    The thyroid is a butterfly-shaped gland that sits low on the front of the neck. Your thyroid lies below your Adam's apple, along the front of the windpipe. The thyroid     has two side lobes, connected by a bridge (isthmus) in the middle.Thyroid nodules are lump like formation in the thyroid which might be cancerous in heavy cases.     Generally Radiologists analyse the ultra-sound scan of the thyroid and detect the nodule. For large number of samples it takes time. With TNTD we facilitate the     analysation by introducing a Deep learning model. We use CNN and image processing algorithms to do this task. With this a large number of sample can be analyzed     within a short period of time. Thus, increasing the analyzing frequency significantly. All you need is your scan image and you are ready to go. Upload your scan and get     the result immediately.
